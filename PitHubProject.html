What is PitHub?

PitHub is like GitHub but with the abiltiy to embed videos inside easily. It allows users to upload their videos and store them within the secure cloud. Users will also be able to configure their source code via GitHub and or Git. PitHub makes it easy for teachers, developers, or anyone who wants to share their videos.









Why Choose PitHub?

PitHub is the future for video storage services created by developers. More often than not, many people find issues where their files are corrupted or, for some reason MIA from their devices. PitHub offers a secure way to store, share and access your videos from anywhere in the world.
