What is PitHub?

PitHub is like GitHub but with the ability to embed videos inside easily. It allows users to upload their videos and store them within the secure cloud. Users will also configure their source code via GitHub and or Git. Your time is valued, and PitHub makes it easy for teachers, developers, or anyone who wants to share their code along with videos quickly.









Why Choose PitHub?

PitHub is the future for video storage services created by developers. Store your video demos along with your code in one place. Spend less time logging into different systems and can share one link for your demo videos. PitHub offers a secure way to share code and videos and access from anywhere in the world.
