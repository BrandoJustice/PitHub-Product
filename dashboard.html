PitHub Product

You may be asking yourself, "What is PitHub?". Well, let us answer that question for you. PitHub is a video-sharing service that allows users to upload their own videos. These videos are to be stored on our cloud database where they are easily accessible. We will also have the capability to lets users edit their source code video via Git or GitHub. Think of us as your go-to place to safely store your videos.


Currently not all of these features are readily available but we will inform you, our cherished customers, when they're finalized!
